My Hobbies & Interests

Welcome! Here’s a peek at what I get up to when I’m not working or studying—my hobbies, my way of having fun.

Hobby 1: Hiking

I started hiking during my senior year of high school. At first, it was just a way to get outside and clear my head, but I quickly started to love it. I love the feeling of pushing myself up a trail and then getting to just stop and look at the beautiful view. Over the years, I’ve done everything from easy walks to trails that totally killed me, and each one has its own memories, like beautiful sunsets or just laughing through a tough trail with my friends and family. Hiking’s definitely helped me grow too, teaching me patience, sticking with something even when it’s hard, and just enjoying being out in nature.

Hobby 2: Wake Surfing

I got into wake surfing because one of my best friend made me try it, and honestly, I’m so glad she did. At first, I could barely stay on the board, but I ended up loving the feeling of gliding across the water, and feeling it splash against my face. It’s just so fun, keeps me moving, and I love being out there with friends, and listening to music while everyone does it. Over time, I’ve gotten better at staying up and even trying a few tricks…though that picture? Yep, that’s me totally biffing it.

Hobby 3: Spending time with my family

Spending time with my family is hands-down my favorite hobby. I love just being with them, whether we’re going on little adventures, cooking together, or just laughing over random stories. It’s the time when I feel the most relaxed and myself, and it’s honestly the most important thing to me. These moments have taught me a lot about patience, understanding, and appreciating the little things in life, and no matter what else I’m doing, I always look forward to our time together.
